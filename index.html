About Emma

My Story

Hey there! My name is Emma Stefania, and I am a writer from Brooklyn, New York.

I envisioned life would unfold for me in its predictable way. I went to college and received a Bachelor of Arts in International Studies and Master of Science in Journalism. I had big dreams and high expectations, and I was certain I knew the way to realizing my ambitions until God stopped me in my tracks and guided me to where I needed to be.

A few years ago, I was led on a spiritual journey that would – and continues to – shape my life in profoundly transformative ways. Once I started on this path, I could never turn back.

I have always been interested in mysticism. As a truth seeker, I enjoy exploring different spiritual schools of thought which endeavor to explain the Unseen reality beyond the veil and the deeper truth underlying existence. At a point, it became more than just a matter of academic inquiry; I was experiencing a shift in focus from the material to the spiritual.

Writing is the outlet for me to creatively express that which I experience and to help others who share similar difficulties. It’s become a way for me to acknowledge the pains of the human condition while embracing the wisdom found in the darkest of moments.

My intention is to help others live their lives more consciously, and through the written word, plant seeds of faith in the real power of spiritual awareness. I am a firm believer in the idea that change first starts within.

In my writing, I bridge the physical and spiritual through the wisdom of experience in the hopes of fostering understanding and healing as well as providing assurance that a more conscious path is possible.
